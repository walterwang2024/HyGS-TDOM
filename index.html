Overview

This project proposes HyGS-TDOM, a hybrid framework for true digital orthophoto map generation by combining 3D Gaussian Splatting and few-shot view synthesis. Our approach tackles the challenges in sparse-view UAV photogrammetry by leveraging FSGS-based representations to boost quality near boundary areas.
